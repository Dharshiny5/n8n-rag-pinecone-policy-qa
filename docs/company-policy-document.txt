COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT

COMPANY OVERVIEW:
This document outlines the policies, procedures, and service guidelines of the company. These policies are designed to ensure transparency, consistency, and high-quality customer service.

RETURN POLICY:
- Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt.
- Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in resalable condition.
- Returns requested after 30 days will not be accepted unless approved under special circumstances.

REFUND POLICY:
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned product is received and inspected.
- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
- Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to company error.

EXCHANGE POLICY:
- Items can be exchanged within 15 days if they are defective, damaged, or incorrect.
- Customers must provide proof of purchase and evidence of the issue.
- Exchanges are subject to product availability.

PRICING AND DISCOUNT POLICY:
- Orders above 100 units receive a 10% discount.
- Orders above 500 units receive a 15% discount.
- Seasonal promotions may apply additional discounts.
- Prices are subject to change without prior notice.

SHIPPING POLICY:
- Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days.
- Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation.
- Customers will receive tracking details once the order is dispatched.

EXPRESS SHIPPING:
- Express delivery is available within 1–2 business days for an additional fee.
- Availability depends on location and product stock.

SHIPPING CHARGES:
- Free shipping for orders above £50.
- Standard shipping fees apply to smaller orders.
- International shipping charges vary by destination.

ORDER CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement.
- Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled but may be returned.

WARRANTY POLICY:
All products come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover:
- Physical damage
- Misuse
- Unauthorized repairs

INVOICE & BILLING:
- Invoices are generated automatically after order confirmation.
- Customers receive invoices via email.

PRODUCT AVAILABILITY:
- Product availability depends on stock levels and is updated regularly.
- Out-of-stock items may be restocked or discontinued.

LATE DELIVERY POLICY:
In case of delays:
- Customers will be notified.
- Compensation may be offered depending on the situation.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING:
- International shipping is available.
- Delivery time and charges depend on destination and customs processing.

DATA PRIVACY:
- Customer data is securely stored and not shared with third parties without consent.
- The company complies with data protection regulations.
